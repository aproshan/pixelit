Escape in Space

An Endless Runner Game

Game Description

In "Escape in Space," you take on the role of a rocket navigating through a cosmic battlefield. Your mission is to evade falling aliens while collecting power-ups to boost your score. The game features an endless runner format, ensuring that the challenge never stops!

Gameplay Mechanics

Players control the rocket by tilting their phone using the gyroscope, dodging aliens that descend from above. The longer you survive, the higher your score will be. Compete against your friends to set the highest score and prove your skills!

High Score

Think you have what it takes to set a new high score? Keep playing and try to beat your previous records. Share your scores with friends and challenge them to do better!
